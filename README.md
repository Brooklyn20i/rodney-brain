# Rodney's Brain

A personal knowledge base built from curated reading on AI, leadership, technology, and commercial transformation.

## Structure

```
articles/     — Individual article summaries with full source text
concepts/     — Synthesised concept pages (built weekly)
INDEX.md      — Master catalog of all articles
```

## How it works

Articles are automatically ingested from a Gmail-based capture system. Every post or article forwarded with "brain food" in the subject becomes a structured markdown file here within hours.

- Daily digest emails synthesise the library at 5am
- Weekly concept pages are rebuilt every Sunday
- Weekly lint reports identify themes, contradictions, and gaps
