The day, run like an operator.

Cadence Work is an executive cockpit, not a to-do list: it separates what needs you from what you're waiting on and what your agent is already doing.

01 · CONTROL

Three buckets, zero ambiguity.

Every morning, Control answers one question: what actually needs me today?

  • My to-do — decisions flagged apart from tasks, with due dates and projects attached
  • Waiting on others — every commitment owed to you, held against a person, so follow-up is a glance not a memory
  • With Kobe — tasks you've delegated to your agent, visible until they're done

02 · BOARD & PEOPLE

Work lives against people, not just lists.

The Board shows everything in flight by owner. Slide a card to reassign; mark it “for Kobe” and it lands in your agent's queue like any teammate's.

  • People pages hold 1:1 agendas, waiting-fors and meeting history in one place
  • Meetings capture actions straight onto the board — nothing evaporates after the call
03 · HORIZON

Projects on a timeline you can defend.

Milestones, target dates and project health on a live Gantt — the view you'd build in a slide, always current instead.

  • Project health (red / amber / green) with a named next action — no zombie projects
  • A weekly review ritual that closes loops and files the week
