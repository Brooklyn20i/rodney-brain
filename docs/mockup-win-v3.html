Enabling Commercial to WIN

Commercial Technology Strategy

Winning Aspiration

Make Commercial teams more effective and Commercial outcomes stronger through technology, AI, and agentic workflows.

Core message

Commercial has a clear strategy. The Commercial Technology strategy exists to enable it through technology, AI and better ways of working at scale. This strategy has a clear focus on fixing the basics:

  • Fix the core workflows, data, and tools that slow Commercial down today
  • Make decisions better with embedded insight and AI support
  • Move from manual execution to agentic workflows where AI is built into how work gets done

This is not a stand-alone technology agenda. It is designed to enable the overarching Commercial strategy and support the Commercial community IPO serves.

1 · Winning aspiration — we will do this by

  • Fixing the basics in the workflows people rely on every day
  • Improving decision quality in the moments that matter most, with insight and AI built into the workflow
  • Shifting key workflows to an agentic model where AI drafts, structures and automates routine work and people stay accountable for judgement and sign off
  • Reducing effort and coordination overhead by removing manual steps, duplication and avoidable handoffs
  • Simplifying and improving the user experience for all end users
  • Scaling common ways of working across markets where it creates value, with clear flexibility where it does not

Success is not systems delivered. Success is Commercial work that is:

  • Easier to run
  • Faster end to end
  • More consistent at the decision points
  • Increasingly agentic in the workflows that matter, not just individual productivity hacks
  • Measurably improved through adoption, automation, lead time reduction, and capacity released

3 · How we will win — four shifts

Move from unlimited demand to disciplined portfolio trade-offs. Managed as a constrained portfolio, not an accumulation of projects.
Technology as a broad activity portfolio→A focused portfolio aligned to Commercial outcomes and explicit trade-offs
Waiting for platform solutions before delivering value→Delivering value early while building scalable capability (Stream 1 solutions & Project Phoenix)
Fragmented local ways of working→Harmonised priority workflows with sensible flexibility
Go-live as proof of success→Adoption, capacity release and faster execution as proof of value
Core operating rule: No new demand starts unless something else stops. Enforced through one in, one out portfolio trade-offs.
Governance linkage: The Commercial Technology Council is the single joint forum where Commercial ambition is translated into Technology direction, and prioritised decisions are fed into TPC and EBC.
