**The Smart Ape (@the_smart_ape) · April 12, 2026 · 271K Views · 784 Likes · 2.9K Bookmarks**

**claude code + obsidian + skill graph. how to build a local research engine?**

i built a research system for 4 clients in the past 6 months. one media company, two consulting firms, and one independent analyst. combined, they cut their research costs by about 60%. one of them fired 3 junior researchers and replaced them with this system + one senior editor who reviews the output.

no fancy tools. no $500/mo subscriptions. just a folder of .md files, one ai agent (claude), and a system that takes one research question and turns it into a multi-angle analysis.

**what is a research skill graph?**

a research skill graph is a folder of interconnected markdown files where each file is one node/skill/lens. when you point claude at this folder and give it a research question, it runs the question through 6 different analytical lenses, evaluates sources against a trust tier system, surfaces contradictions on purpose, and synthesizes findings into structured output.

the difference: a single prompt gives you a summary. a skill graph gives you a research department.

**why 6 lenses instead of one big prompt?**

- **technical:** what do the numbers actually say? look at data only
- **economic:** follow the money. who pays, who profits, what incentives drive behavior
- **historical:** what patterns repeat? what's been tried before? what context is everyone forgetting
- **geopolitical:** zoom out to the global chessboard. which countries, which power dynamics
- **contrarian:** what if the consensus is wrong? who benefits from the current narrative
- **first principles:** forget everything. rebuild from fundamental truths only

each lens produces findings that often contradict the others. and that tension between lenses is where the real insight lives.

**the compound effect**

knowledge/concepts.md and knowledge/data-points.md accumulate across ALL your research projects. after 10 projects, your claude agent has a deep base of verified facts, patterns, and concepts to pull from. the open-questions from one research become the index.md of the next. the system self-generates future research.

**how to use it**

- Method 1: Claude Projects (recommended) — create a new project, upload all files, give it a topic
- Method 2: Paste context — copy index.md + relevant lens files into any AI chat
- Method 3: Claude Code + Obsidian (most powerful) — point claude code at your local vault. the agent reads, writes, and updates files autonomously.
