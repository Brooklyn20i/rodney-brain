*Complete original content archived below for reference.*

**Don't type another prompt into Claude. Do these 9 simple things first:**

1. **Download the Claude app** — Claude.ai works. But the desktop app is better. Go to claude.com/download.
2. **Pick the Right Model** — Select Opus 4.6. Turn on Extended Thinking.
3. **Use Cowork, Not Chat** — Cowork reads your actual files. Chat doesn't. Point Claude to your project folder.
4. **Build Your Folder Structure** — Create 4 folders: ABOUT ME, PROJECTS, TEMPLATES, CLAUDE OUTPUTS.
5. **Write Your "About Me" md File** — .md file about who you are, your work, thinking. Claude reads it before every response.
6. **Write Your Anti-AI Style File** — A .md file listing every word & pattern you refuse. Ban "delve," "landscape," "leverage."
7. **Start Asking** — End prompt with: "Ask me clarifying questions first." The output jumps 2–3 levels.
8. **Set Up Global Instructions** — Go to Settings → Profile → Global Instructions. Claude reads this before every single conversation.
9. **Edit Your Prompt** — Don't send a new message. Edit the first one. Every follow-up eats tokens. Editing keeps your context clean.
